Ademar was an 11 year old boy with the diagnosis of epilepsy when I met him in January, 2005. His story is tragic in that his mother developed ‘eclampsia’ (severe hypertension and seizures) during pregnancy, and died from a stroke shortly after Ademar’s birth. Because of his mother’s condition, Ademar’s birth was problematic and his brain likely suffered from a lack of oxygen during his prolonged delivery, resulting in seizures that began in infancy.  He is under the care of his very loving grandmother.

When I met Ademar, he was having seizures almost every day. Arrangements were made for evaluation by a neurologist.. He was started on anti-seizure medications, after which his seizures have been almost completely eliminated.

Ademar is considered to be a quiet, passive boy amongst strangers.  However, among his family, he is thought of as obedient, helpful and always fun to be with. My interview with the grandmother was difficult to complete due to her constant weeping… she would like for me to express her sincere gratitude to you, the donors.  She says that you have “made life so much better for Ademar”.
