Dr. Jonathan Lee-Melk with children from Guardaria Hasta Crecer
Until Grown Daycare


Guardia Hasta Crecer is an institution that provides daytime food, education, and supervision for children of families that live in extreme poverty.  This service allows the parents to work during the day without leaving their
children unattended at home.  

The RVF supports regular visits to Guardaria Hasta Crecer by community health workers to provide
prevention services, including vaccinations and weight checks.  The families of these children may also seek
curative health services at the clinic at minimal or no cost.
