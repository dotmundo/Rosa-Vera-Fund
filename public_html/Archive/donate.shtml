Thank you for considering a generous donation to The Rosa Vera Fund. As you determine the amount you want to invest in this project, remember the following:

  • Completing quality prevention and medical work in Bolivia is incredibly inexpensive.
  • The Rosa Vera Fund's interventions are entirely dependent on the donations of individuals, families and groups. There is no other source of funding.
  • 100% of each donation is utilized to fund preventive or curative interventions. No administrative fees are deducted!
  • Donations are tax-deductible.
  • Regular reports are provided.
