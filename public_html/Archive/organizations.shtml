Our Partner Organizations

Curamericas

This nonprofit organization, headquartered in Raleigh, North Carolina, has provided primary health care and practical health education to women and children living in poverty throughout Latin America.

El Consejo de Salud Rural Andino (CSRA)

The Rural Andean Health Counsel is a Bolivian nonprofit organization that reduces the morbidity/mortality of poverty-stricken communities throughout Bolivia.

 

CSRA and Curamericas have worked together in Montero since 1983 to provide: Collaborations include:
  • child survival techniques
  • maternal/ reproductive health
  • micro-enterprise development
  • water sanitation
  • tuberculosis intervention
  • The US Agency for International Development
  • The Center for Disease Control and Prevention
  • CARE International
  • Freedom From Hunger
  • Water for People

 

These efforts have established some of the region's largest and most successful health care initiatives.
