Wilber lives with his parents and four siblings deep in the countryside where laborers subsist by baking bricks from mud.  Homes are without electricity or potable water; distance and poverty prevent families from seeking preventive or curative treatments.  The RVF helps reach children in this isolated community by financially supporting the efforts of the Community Health Worker Program.

On the day this photo was taken, Wilber was vaccinated for the first time; his mother received nutritional counseling and fortified cooking oil.
