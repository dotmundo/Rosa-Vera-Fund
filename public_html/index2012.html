Memorial Celebrating Joseph Andriola

Joe Andriola, a dear friend and strong supporter of the Rosa Vera Fund, died tragically in a car accident while traveling in China in January. Joe’s wife Judy Carty asked that in lieu of flowers, donations be made to the Rosa Vera Fund in Joe’s honor.

Recent RVF Retreat

Drs. Lee-Melk, Ray and Hunt met with the RVF Board of Directors for the first annual Board Retreat. The all-day event included in-depth presentations on RVF patients, community health workers and scholarship recipients, as well as discussions of the mission statement, communications and fundraising strategy, and budget going forward. It was a fascinating and informative day for all. The exciting news, shared at all the events of the week, is the upcoming groundbreaking for a school/therapy center that RVF and CSRA are building in Montero, made possible by donations in memory of Joseph Andriola, and by The Gould Foundation.
