We have received news from Bolivia that María Fernanda passed away on the afternoon of February 6th.

Last year we initiated a special fundraising effort to pay for María Fernanda's chemotherapy and other medical treatments related to her leukemia.

Our Bolivian partners and MF's parents wanted to express their deepest appreciation for the efforts of everyone who contributed to Maria's care. They write:

"We are genuinely shocked by this outcome – our affection for Maria Fernanda makes it hard to understand what has happened, why it happened, how we failed. We can't find answers, which leaves us deeply frustrated since we held so much hope for saving the life of this courageous girl who day after day fought to overcome this illness that was consuming her.
 
What we do know with certainty is that the great financial aid that was offered provided Maria Fernanda with a higher quality of life, a more dignified life, better nutrition, and the opportunity to be a young girl—a happy girl. This, at least, gives us some comfort."
