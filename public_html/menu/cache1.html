4/16/2011 Spring update: Teaching Design Build studio at Yale has been insightful, some of the most intelligent and hardworking first year students I have encountered as an educator; a great group! I have also had the pleasure of teaching with certain extraordinary individuals who are some of the most passionate educators I have known over the past 9 years of teaching. A total pleasure. At TMA we are finishing up an interiors project in South Beach which has been simply refreshing - can't wait to get them up photographed and up on the site! Lastly, I have finally started twitting again....current topic is 'urban notables'; design and architectural discoveries (good and bad) that I find while walking the streets of New York...just for fun.(Now to get that twitter badge on the website....)

4/6/09 TINA MANIS ASSOCIATES TO DESIGN THE ART FUND PAVILION IN LONDON, UK: The Lightbox and Tent London has selected Tina Manis Associates from among a group of five finalists to design The Art Fund Pavilion. The Pavilion will be engineered and constructed by Facit and shown as part of Tent London during the London Design Festival 2009, before taking up residence at The Lightbox as an annual summer pavilion and gallery space.

3/17/09 WE ARE PLEASED TO ANNOUNCE OUR SHORTLISTED ENTRY: The Art Fund Pavilion open competition presented by Tent London and the Lightbox, has selected Tina Manis Associates’ submission as one of its five shortlisted projects from over 600 hundred submissions from 52 countries for a semi-permanent pavilion. TMA’s entry was the only selected finalist representing the United States.
