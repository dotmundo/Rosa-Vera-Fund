HOST AN EVENT
Most of our donors come by word of mouth. If you would like to help, you can host a party/information session/fundraiser at your home or office. We will be happy to present a short slide presentation to your guests, and answer any questions they may have. We can work with any event, large or small, i.e. birthday party, office luncheon, cocktail hour, or casual gathering. Contact Karen Melk at Karen.melk@gmail.com for more information.

TELL YOUR FRIENDS
Let your friends know about us. Ask them to donate to a good cause for holidays or birthdays instead of buying gifts. Their money will go a lot further and help change a life.
